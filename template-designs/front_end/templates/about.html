About Us

Bus-Ticket is an online ticket booking system that will save your precious time, money and energy by providing you with the ability to search and find buses along with their services.

Buses at bus-ticket focus on passengers satisfaction in order to provide high quality service by using international measurement methods and improvement mechanisms to ensure the Word Class travels services are provided to it's passenges.

Who We Are?

Bus-ticket is completely independent company that provides services
related safe travel. We help our clients to save their resources by
finding the services they need online.
The idea came from the inspiration that an online Bus ticket booking
system would help improve transport access for many.
This was because the country where the developers were based,
Ethiopia, did not have this service.
